Why the build drifted — and the fix
The left screen is the target. Compare it to the current build. Five root causes:
1
Use the illustrated background image — do not rebuild the scene from shapes
The build drew flat mountain bands + box "buildings". Instead, render one produced art asset (location_village_day.webp) full-bleed as the bottom layer. All the village warmth lives in that image.
2
Characters must be grounded, never floating
Guides currently hover in the sky with leftover translucent "ghost" circles behind them. Either bake guides into the scene art, or anchor each sprite so its feet sit on the ground with a small contact shadow — and delete the placeholder background circle.
3
Subject launchers are cream cards, not gray disabled circles
The "Daily Challenge" strip renders as dead gray circles that look disabled. Each destination is a cream #FFF7E8 card with a colored icon badge, name, subject label, and a progress bar in the subject color.
4
Legible tags: cream fill + ink text + progress + lock state
Current tags are dark-brown boxes with tiny low-contrast text. Use the cream card above; locked stops get a gray card + 🔒 + "Reach Level 15" — never signal locked by color alone.
5
Restore the top-bar profile card + XP bar & tactile buttons
The build lost the avatar/level/XP card and shipped a bare version string. Restore the cream profile card with the gold XP bar, and give every button the chunky bottom shadow (0 5px 0 darker) so it reads as pressable.
Layer order (bottom → top)
1 · illustrated backdrop image
2 · optional grounded building + guide sprites (if not baked into the image)
3 · dark→transparent scrim top & bottom for legibility
4 · native chrome: top bar, Daily Quest, destination tags, bottom nav
Full step-by-step in handoff/docs/village-home-build-spec.md.
